BreakOut

Game options:

Rules:

Use the arrows keys to move the paddle along the bottom of the screen left and right.
Hit the ball with the paddle to break up the cubes at the top of the screen. If the ball passes your paddle you lose!
Win the game by breaking up all the cubes with the ball!

Keyboard inputs:

Enter: start the game.
Spacebar: pause & unpause.
R: resets the game.
Arrow key left: move the paddle to the left.
Arrow key right: move the paddle to the right.
