Snake

Game Options

Modes & Options:

Game mode Apples (min 1 max 25)
Obstacles (min 0 max 25)
Grid tile units X & Y (min 8 max 32) and must be divisible by 2
Speed increase when eating appple (min 0 max 10)
Snakes teleport around screen
Place Portals

Rules:

Snake must eat apples to grow while avoiding itself, other snakes and obstacles.
If you grow up until you fill the game screen you win.
If you choose 2 Player or AI mode the surviving snake left wins the game.
If you choose doubble snake you lose the game if one of the 2 snakes dies.

Game Objects:

Red squares are apples that grow the snake.
Blue square are obstacles that kills the snake.
Yellow squares are portals that teleports the snake.
A snake is made out of green scquares.
A snake head is a single dark green square.
A snake body is a green square + a smaller & lighter green square.

Keyboard inputs:

Enter: start the game.
Spacebar: pause & unpause.
R: resets the game.
Arrow keys: Player 1 up, down, left & right control.
WASD keys: Player 2 up, down, left & right control.

Mouse inputs:

You can also mouse click the screen to move Player 1.
The screen is split into 4 triagles by an X.
Each triangle is a screen relative direction.
Clicking at the start screen will start the game.
Clicking at the gameOver screen will reset the game.
